Social Trend Analysis

Social Trend Analysis is the project we have done to fulfill the requirements of the course Knowledge Discovery Management for Spring 2014. As many operating systems are being used in current days, we thought to find out the popular operating systems in different parts of the world.

Analysing popular OS

We thought to run data analytics on the data pulled from the social networks like twitter. We pulled raw data from twitter, then we cleansed our data and we run data analytics on the cleansed and processed data. We got the output of the popular Operating systems in different places around the world. We represented those data through Maps and charts.

Scope

Scope of our Social Trend Analysis Project is we can work on finding out the trends of the operating systems popularity over the period of time. We can work on comparing the operating systems with their prices over the period of time. We can run recommendation algorithms to recommend the operating systems to a user.
